Privacy Policy for AI Chat Enhancer

Last updated: May 9, 2026

No Data Collection

AI Chat Enhancer does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, browsing history, or user data to any external server or third party.

Local Storage Only

All data (prompt templates, folder names, and usage count) is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API. This data never leaves your browser and is deleted when you uninstall the extension.

No Analytics, No Tracking

This extension does not use analytics, tracking scripts, cookies, or any form of user monitoring. No third-party services receive any data from this extension.

Pro Subscription

Pro activation codes are verified locally. Payment processing is handled entirely by Payhip, a third-party platform. Payhip may collect payment information per their own privacy policy.
